Pancakes recipe



Description

Simple yet still one of the best foods in the world.

Ingredients

  • Flour 6 spoons
  • Milk ~1 cup
  • Egg 1
  • Salt a pinch

Steps

  1. Mixing

    Stir the egg until yolk and egg white mix together.

  2. Mixing

    Put 3 spoons of flour pour milk from eye and mix everything together repeat until you put all the flour.

  3. Mixing

    Put a pinch of salt and mix it into the dought.

  4. Cooking

    Fry the pancakes with oil.

    Enjoy!!!
