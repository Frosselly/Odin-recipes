Hotdog recipe



Description

A great recipe for getting that for growing that belly.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Hotdog bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Cooking

    Fry the sausage with oil.

  2. Building

    Put the sausage into the bun.

  3. Building

    Put mustard and ketchup on the hotdog.

    Enjoy!!!
