Burrito recipe



Description

Easy recipe with high nutrition and very delicious filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • Your choice meat
  • Chopped Onion and grounded garlic (for meat)
  • Spices: cumin paprika ginger salt
  • Tortilla
  • Rice
  • Lettuce
  • Sour cream or yogurt
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Start boiling the rice.
  2. Fry the meat add onion and garlic wait until onion has a golden tint. Add the spices
  3. Add everything together on the tortillia meat, rice, lettuce and yogurt. And also add cheese to help burrito stay together.
  4. Now wrap the burrito and Enjoy!!!
